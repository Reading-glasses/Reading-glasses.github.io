A current and accurate eye prescription is paramount. This document, provided by your optometrist, contains vital information for your **glasses with bifocals**, including your specific vision correction needs for both distance and reading. Crucially, it must include your **pupillary distance (PD)**. PD measures the distance between the centers of your pupils, ensuring the optical center of your **bifocal lenses** aligns perfectly with your eyes for optimal clarity. Many **online optician for bifocals** platforms offer guides or simple online tools to measure your PD if it's not on your prescription, which is essential for proper **understanding bifocal prescriptions**.
