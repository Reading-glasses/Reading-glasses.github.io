If you find yourself holding your phone or book further away to read, experiencing eye fatigue after reading, or struggling with small print in dimly lit environments, these are clear indicators that it might be time to consider **reading glasses**. Many **males with presbyopia** initially dismiss these signs, but addressing them promptly can prevent discomfort and maintain visual acuity.
